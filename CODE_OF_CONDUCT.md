# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

ThreatLens aims to foster a welcoming, respectful, and professional community.

Participants are expected to:

* Be respectful.
* Be constructive.
* Be inclusive.
* Help others learn.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

* Harassment
* Personal attacks
* Hate speech
* Disruptive conduct

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to maintain a positive environment.
